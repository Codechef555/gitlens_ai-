# GitLens AI — 6-slide pitch

## 1. The problem

Engineers spend hours or days developing a mental model of an unfamiliar repository.

## 2. The product

GitLens AI imports a codebase and turns it into architecture, dependency, risk, impact, and chat intelligence.

## 3. Why now

Teams move faster than their documentation. AI can synthesize codebase context into an explorable product surface.

## 4. Product proof

Show the architecture canvas, dependency graph, impact simulator, and repository-aware streamed chat.

## 5. Differentiation

GitLens is not a blank chatbot: it uses structured repository context and visual workflows that support engineering decisions.

## 6. Roadmap

Durable workspaces, GitHub OAuth, semantic indexing, PR intelligence, and team collaboration.
